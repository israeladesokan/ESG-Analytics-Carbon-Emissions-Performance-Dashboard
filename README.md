# ESG Analytics & Carbon Emissions Performance Dashboard

### Executive Sustainability Reporting & Carbon Performance Analytics for Concrete Manufacturing Operations

## Project Overview

The dashboard was developed using a concrete batching plant dataset containing production, cement consumption, energy usage, diesel consumption, renewable electricity adoption, and CO₂ emissions metrics. The objective was to simulate a real-world sustainability reporting environment used by ESG teams, Sustainability Managers, and executive leadership.

---

## Business Problem

Concrete manufacturing is one of the most carbon-intensive industrial sectors globally due to its dependence on cement production, fossil fuel consumption, and energy-intensive operations.

Industrial organizations face increasing pressure to:

- Reduce carbon emissions
- Improve energy efficiency
- Increase renewable energy utilization
- Meet ESG reporting requirements
- Support sustainability targets and decarbonization strategies

This project demonstrates how operational and environmental data can be integrated into a decision-support dashboard for sustainability performance management.

---

## Dashboard Objectives

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor carbon emissions performance
- Track operational efficiency
- Analyze energy and fuel consumption
- Evaluate renewable energy adoption
- Benchmark plant sustainability performance
- Support ESG reporting and carbon reduction initiatives
- Identify operational improvement opportunities
- Monitor sustainability targets and KPIs


---

## Key Sustainability KPIs

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Total CO₂ Emissions | Total carbon footprint generated across operations |
| CO₂ Intensity (tCO₂/m³) | Carbon emissions per cubic meter of concrete produced |
| Renewable Energy Ratio | Share of electricity sourced from renewable energy |
| Energy Intensity | Electricity consumption per unit of production |
| Fuel Intensity | Diesel consumption per unit of production |
| Cement Emissions Contribution | Carbon emissions associated with cement usage |
| Plant Sustainability Ranking | Comparative sustainability performance across plants |
| ESG Target Achievement | Progress against sustainability targets |

---

## Key Insights Generated

The analysis highlights several sustainability and operational insights:

- Cement manufacturing is the dominant contributor to total carbon emissions.
- Renewable electricity adoption varies significantly across plants.
- Carbon intensity differs by plant type and operational efficiency.
- Diesel consumption remains a major contributor to operational emissions.
- Opportunities exist to reduce emissions through increased renewable energy utilization and process optimization.

---

## ESG Framework Alignment

The dashboard aligns with widely adopted sustainability reporting frameworks and carbon accounting standards:

- GHG Protocol
- Scope 1 Emissions Reporting
- Scope 2 Emissions Reporting
- Scope 3 Emissions Reporting
- GRI 305 Emissions Standards
- Corporate Sustainability Reporting Practices
